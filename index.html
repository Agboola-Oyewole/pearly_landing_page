Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Campus Connect

How do I sign up for Campus Connect?
Sign up is easy! Download the app, create an account using your university email address, and verify your student status. This ensures all users are part of your campus community.
Does Campus Connect handle payments or deliveries?
Campus Connect does not process payments or deliveries. Buyers and sellers communicate through chat to agree on prices, payment methods, and meetups independently.
Is Campus Connect available on iPhone?
Yes! You can access Campus Connect on iPhone through our web app. Simply open the site in Safari and tap "Add to Home Screen" for a native app-like experience. The Android app is also available for download.
How do I list an item for sale?
Listing items is simple! Tap the "+" button, upload photos of your item, add a title and description, set your price, and publish. Your listing will be visible to all verified students in your university.
How does the messaging system work?
When you're interested in an item, you can message the seller directly through the app. All conversations are secure and stay within Campus Connect. You'll receive notifications when someone replies to your messages.
Are there any fees for buying or selling?
No! Campus Connect is completely free to use currently. There are no listing fees, transaction fees, or hidden charges at the moment. We believe students should be able to trade freely within their community.
How do I arrange a safe meetup?
We recommend meeting in public places on campus during daytime hours. Always bring a friend if possible, and trust your instincts.
What items can I sell on Campus Connect?
You can sell textbooks, electronics, furniture, clothing, sports equipment, and more. Items must be legal and comply with university policies. Prohibited items include weapons, drugs, and counterfeit goods.
How does the rating system work?
After completing a transaction, both buyers and sellers can rate each other. Build your reputation by being responsive, honest, and reliable. A good rating helps other students trust you for future trades.
Can I report inappropriate content or users?
Yes. If you encounter inappropriate listings, scams, or suspicious behavior, you can report them directly through the app. Our team reviews all reports and takes appropriate action to keep the community safe.
How do I contact support?
Email us at fesconnectapp@gmail.com. We typically respond within 24 hours. Check our Help Center for instant answers to common questions.
