Privacy Policy

Peerly · Last updated June 17, 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains what information Peerly collects, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using Peerly you agree to this Policy.

2. Information you provide

When you sign up and use Peerly we collect information such as your name, email address, university email, matric/student number, phone number, university, department, profile photo, and the listings, messages, reviews, and contact details (e.g. WhatsApp number, website) you choose to add.

3. Information collected automatically

We collect limited technical and usage data to run and improve the Service — for example device and app information, log data, approximate activity (such as listings viewed), and push-notification tokens so we can deliver notifications.

4. How we use your information

We use your information to create and secure your account, verify student status, show your profile and listings to other users, enable chat and notifications, power search and recommendations, prevent fraud and abuse, provide support, and comply with the law.

5. What other users can see

Your name, username, profile photo, verification status, ratings, active listings, and any contact details you add (such as a WhatsApp number or website) are visible to other users so the marketplace can work. Do not put information in your listings or messages that you don't want others to see.

6. Service providers

We use trusted third-party services to operate Peerly — including Google Firebase (authentication, database, storage, hosting, cloud functions, messaging), Google Sign-In, and an email provider used to send verification messages. These providers process data on our behalf under their own terms and security practices.

7. Storage and security

Your data is stored using Google Firebase infrastructure. We take reasonable measures to protect it, but no method of transmission or storage is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Data retention

We keep your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the Service, resolve disputes, and meet legal obligations. You can request deletion of your account and associated personal data by contacting us.

9. Your choices and rights

You can view and update most profile information in the app, control notifications through your device settings, and request access to or deletion of your personal data by contacting us. We'll respond as required by applicable law.

10. Children

Peerly is intended for university students and is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age required in your country). We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under that age.

11. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. We'll change the "Last updated" date and, where appropriate, notify you in the app.

12. Contact

Questions or requests about your privacy? Email support@peerly.app.
